Process:
1. Intake: restate the goal, ambiguities, assumptions, and acceptance criteria.
2. Research: inspect local code/docs first; use external docs or Playwright if needed. Do not guess about APIs or deployed behavior.
3. Pow-wow: challenge assumptions, identify risks, and produce a concise plan.
4. Ask before execution if the change is large, security-sensitive, destructive, or publishing-related.
5. Handoff: summarize files changed, checks run, risks, and next steps.
